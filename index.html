Tolerance Reset Guide
⏱️
Full reset takes ~10 days of zero caffeine. Adenosine receptors fully upregulate back to baseline. First 2-3 days are the hardest (headaches, fatigue).
📉
Gradual taper is easier: reduce by 10-25% every 2 days. Minimizes withdrawal. Takes ~2-3 weeks but much more sustainable for athletes.
💪
For athletic performance, keep caffeine for key training days only. 3-6 mg/kg body weight pre-workout is the evidence-based dose. Reserve it for races and hard sessions.
😴
Sleep hygiene matters: avoid caffeine after 14:00 for optimal sleep. Even 6h half-life means a 200mg coffee at 15:00 leaves ~100mg in your blood at 20:00.
🧬
Genetics play a role: CYP1A2 fast metabolizers clear caffeine in ~3-4h, slow metabolizers in 6-9h. If sleep is poor even with cutoff, you may be a slow metabolizer.
